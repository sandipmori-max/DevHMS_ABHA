Based on the permissions declared in the Application Manifest, the Application may collect additional data with your explicit permission to provide functionality:

1. Location Data (Foreground & Background)

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION
  • ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION
  • ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE_LOCATION

The Application may collect your precise or approximate location even when running in the background to provide continuous tracking features, location-based services, and related functionality.

2. Photos, Videos & Media Access

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES
  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO
  • READ_MEDIA_AUDIO
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

With your permission, the Application may access photos, videos, documents, and other files stored on your device. This is necessary for uploading media files, attaching documents, scanning images, etc.

3. Camera Access

The Application requests access to your device's camera to allow capturing photos, scanning documents, or other camera-based features.

4. Audio Recording

The Application may request access to your microphone (RECORD_AUDIO) to support optional features such as voice input or audio-related functionality.

5. Push Notifications

We may request permission to send push notifications for updates, alerts, reminders, or system messages.

6. Auto-Start on Boot

The Application may restart background location services after reboot using:

  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED

This ensures continuous tracking or service continuity where required.
